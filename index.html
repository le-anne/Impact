1. What services do you offer?

We provide tax preparation, bookkeeping, and consulting services for indivuduals, small business, freelancers, contractors, including those who have equity based compensation like RSUs, ISOs, NSOs, and NQs.

2. Do I need to bring any documents with me to the consultation?

Yes! Please remember to bring relevant documents and last year's tax return. This includes any W-2s, 1099s, investment or income statments or documents that you may have received for the current tax year. We also offer virtual appointments and consults, so please have your documents on hand for the appointment.

3. What sets you apart from other tax professionals?

Honestly, not much! We all follow the same IRS guidelines and strive to ensure accuracy and compliance. What matters most is finding a tax advisor who’s a good fit for you. Just like in tech jobs, the culture fit plays a big role. At Summit on Sutter, we’re committed to doing right by you and ensuring your safety from any IRS issues. Our priority is making sure you feel confident and secure throughout the tax process.

4. What types of clients do you work with?

We specialize in working with individuals, small business owners, freelancers, and tech professionals, especially those dealing with equity compensation like RSUs and stock options.

5. How much will your services cost?

Pricing depends on the complexity of your tax situation. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees for any additional form.
